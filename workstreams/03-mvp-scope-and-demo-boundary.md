# Workstream 03 - MVP Scope And Demo Boundary

## V0 Scope

- English only.
- Urgent-care lite.
- One kiosk workflow.
- CPU-only by default.
- Touch input plus optional ASR.
- Limited vital-sign set.
- Small symptom-category set.
- No diagnosis.
- No autonomous medical decision.
- Dynamic questioning.
- Vital-aware routing.
- Structured summary for clinician review.

## Not V0

- All-specialty clinical coverage.
- Production HIS / EMR writeback.
- Real patient data.
- Clinical threshold claims without source verification.
- FDA product claims.
- Full ASR / LLM architecture disclosure.
- Diagnosis or emergency medical advice.

AI-assisted intake and triage-support workflow.
